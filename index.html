About Me

UI/UX Designer

I'm Kiran S Poojary, currently pursuing my MCA at Mangalore Institute of Technology and Engineering with a strong passion for technology and user experience design. I have gained practical experience through various projects, including designing an online store for branded clothing and working with BCG’s Strategic and Experience Design Team on a banking product. I recently completed a UI/UX virtual internship at CodSoft, where I honed my skills in creating user-centered designs. My participation in national-level hackathons, including the SAP HackFest'24 and the Smart India Hackathon, has further enriched my problem-solving abilities. Driven by a desire to learn and grow, I strive to create impactful digital solutions that enhance user experiences.
